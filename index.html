EXPLORE THE PARAVERSE WORLD

Para monsters are super-powered creatures that live on Paraverse World. Each Para monster has a unique superpower, unique appearance and rarity. There are also super rare Para monsters waiting for you to discover.

MAPS

Worlds with diverse landscapes from deserts, ruined cities, deep seas... help you satisfy the power of discovery. In particular, each map will have interactions with Para monsters with different attributes to increase or limit strength.

GAMEPLAY

Collect, evolve, and trade unique NFT heroes to build the best adventure team. Rise through the ranks to claim tournament rewards!
